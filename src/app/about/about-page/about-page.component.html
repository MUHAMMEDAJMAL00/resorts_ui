Wayanad Resorts began as a family beach house on a quiet curve of coastline. What grew from it was never meant to be the biggest resort on the bay — only the most cared-for. Today, 120 rooms, suites and villas sit low among the palms, arranged so that every guest wakes to the water.

We measure success in returning guests: more than half of our visitors each season have stayed with us before. That is the standard we keep — a place worth coming back to.
